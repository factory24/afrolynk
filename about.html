A Bridge between African and European Start-Up Ecosystem

As the largest center for start-ups and digital solution centers in Europe and Africa, we bring together a community that creates value for good. Facing challenges of society, we're on a search for solutions and drive technology beyond the status if quo, and empower people through education to make a difference
Innovate

Transforming entrepreneurs’ and startups’ ideas into innovative businesses and viable products that are worth investing in. Training, supporting, building, and promoting startups with ready minimum viable products(MVP) and solutions to enter new markets. Workshops and Mentoring.

Connect

Connecting European and African startup ecosystems to network. Showcasing and sharing knowledge within the startup communities through meetups and bringing together entrepreneurs, investors, hubs, accelerators, policy makers and VCs during our Premium Annual Conference.

Accelerate

Disrupting and accelerating startups with newer scaling opportunities and disruptive technologies to grow faster, together with our partners’ networks and close ecosystem, startups may have access to technical support, infrastructure support, and solution accelerating fund.
